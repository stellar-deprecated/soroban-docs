The [multisig wallet example] demonstrates a complex auth scheme with multiple signers that authorizes payments either immediately or in a delayed async fashion.

:::tip Stellar supports [multisig accounts] natively, and multisig accounts may be simpler to manage than a contract driven multisig wallet. :::
